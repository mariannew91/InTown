How it works

For Public

Stop Missing Out

Ever found out about a great local event after it already happened? Or wished you knew about a class or group that was running right on your doorstep? That's exactly why InTown exists.

Everything happening in your town — groups, classes, events, activities — all in one place, posted by real people who live there too.

  • Find things you'd actually enjoy - tell us your interests and we'll show you what's relevant. No scrolling through stuff that doesn't apply to you.

  • Never miss out again - get notified when something new matches your interests or when a group you follow posts an update.

  • Real and local - everything on here is from your town, posted by people and organisations in your community. No national chains, no irrelevant results.

  • Free, always - finding things on InTown costs nothing. It always will.

  • No nonsense - no sponsored posts pushed to the top, no adverts cluttering the feed. Every listing is equal. What you see is genuinely what's there, nothing more.

It takes two minutes to sign up. You might be surprised what's been going on without you.

For Groups

Reach the people who are already looking

Running a group, class, or event is hard enough without also trying to get the word out. A Facebook post gets lost. A flyer gets ignored. And the people most likely to come along have no idea you exist.

InTown puts you in front of local people who are actively looking for something to do — and keeps you there.

  • Free to list - add your group, class, or event in minutes. No cost, no catch. Your listing sits alongside everything else in town, equal and visible.

  • Post like social media - share updates, photos, and news directly to your followers on InTown. People who follow your group get notified automatically.

  • The right audience - everyone on InTown is there because they want to find something local. You're not fighting for attention with cat videos and adverts.

  • Simple to manage - no technical knowledge needed. If you can send a text, you can manage your InTown listing.

  • Grow your following - the more you post, the more people follow. Built in word of mouth.

For businesses and organisations, we also offer affordable upgrades to help you stand out even more. But you'll be surprised what the free listing alone can do.
